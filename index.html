Bel-Air Beach

Bel-Air Beach is located in Bofa, in the Maritime region of Guinea. This remote small beach, with its soft sand lined with palm trees, and its calm turquoise waters, offers some of the most breathtaking landscapes in Guinea.

La Dame du Mali

Located 471 km from the capital precisely in the prefecture of Mali, "La Dame du Mali" is in the heart of Fouta-Djallon, more precisely on Mount Loura, culminating at an altitude of 1500 meters. Naturally carved from stone, a woman of remarkable beauty has overlooked the region for centuries. Thus, from the top of its cliff, it dominates the plain which extends over Senegal. The site of "La dame du Mali" is a must in the Guinea of Wonders, do not hesitate to visit the region to better understand its history.

Cascade de Ditinn

The Ditinn Falls located in the Dalaba prefecture and one of the largest waterfalls in the country, from a height of 80 m it stands majestically over the savannah.
